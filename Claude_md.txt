Sub-Agent Authority Rule:
	• A sub-agent’s maximum authority level equals the spawning Lead Agent’s authority level.
	• A sub-agent cannot exceed the Lead Agent’s tool permissions.
	• A sub-agent cannot exceed the Lead Agent’s workspace scope.
	• A sub-agent cannot access global system context unless explicitly granted by Orchestrator.
	• A sub-agent cannot spawn further sub-agents unless explicitly configured.
	• A sub-agent workspace access is limited to the workspace it was assigned.
	• A sub-agent task access is limited to the task or task batch it was assigned.
	• A sub-agent must pass all normal authority checks.
	• A sub-agent must pass all hard-boundary checks.
	• A sub-agent must create audit logs for every action.
	• Any attempted authority escalation must be blocked and reported.
